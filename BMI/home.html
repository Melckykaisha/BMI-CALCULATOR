Welcome to the BMI Calculator

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple method to estimate whether your weight is within a healthy range for your height. It’s commonly used as a screening tool to identify weight problems for adults and children.

How is BMI Calculated?

The BMI formula is:

BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)2

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5–24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25–29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 and above

Why is BMI Important?

BMI is an indicator of body fatness and can help determine your risk for conditions like heart disease, diabetes, and high blood pressure. However, it’s important to remember that BMI is not a perfect measurement as it doesn’t account for muscle mass, bone density, or overall body composition.
